Saxophone Brands

Yamaha

Yamaha is one of the most reliable brands out of them all, and is a very respected and common choice among saxophone players. If you're on a budget, Yamaha will most likely be your best option, as their cheaper models are still very reliable.

Selmer-Paris

Selmer is another one of the greats when it comes to saxophone brands. Known for their overall quality, Selmer is a clear choice for many professionals and students alike. No matter what model you pick, you'll get a quality saxophone with Selmer.

Yanagisawa

Yanagisawa saxophones are among the highest quality saxophone available, most often used by professionals. The price tag might be a bit higher than most others, but Yanagisawa saxophones are consistently some of the best saxophones out there.

Cannonball

Cannonball saxophones are very unique in the sense that they are definitely the most customizable. Out of all of the saxophone brands, Cannonball has the biggest selections of saxophones that are different colors, finishes, etc. If you want a shiny new black saxophone, it's worth checking out Cannonball, although it's important to remember that while still good, Cannonball saxophones aren't as good as other brands listed above.
